Biomedical Engineering Senior Design

As part of our Senior Design project, we developed a tool in Java to evaluate reading performance of any type of reading passage, and potentially diagnose common reading proficiency issues.

This application is able to determine what words and sentences a user speeds through, or gets stuck on by measuring fixations and saccadic eye movements with scientific analysis.

I developed a heatmap tool that generates a heatmap (seen below) of a user's eye tracking during a reading session.

Relay For Life Check-in Page

Languages used:

  • php
  • Javascript/JQuery
  • HTML/CSS
  • MySQL

I used a variety of languages and frameworks to develop a simple, beautiful, check-in page for participants of Relay For Life of Washington University.

Allows students running the check-in/registration tent to quickly search for a registered participant, determine if they are able to be checked in, or redirected to finish requirements. Users can also sign a waiver (if not already completed).

Made to be secure, fast, responsive, and network-agnostic.

Research

Participated in a research project at the Washington University School of Medicine to study the effect of inhibitor of apoptosis protein (IAP) antagonists, a novel anti-cancer therapy, on bone homeostasis.

Through a form of analysis called bone histomorphometry, I discovered that these therapies increased osteoclast activation, which leads to bone metastasis.

This research helped improve the therapies given to cancer patients by using them in conjunction with conventional bisphosphonate treatments for bone deficiencies
