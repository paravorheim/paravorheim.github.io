Hi! I'm Paras Vora.

I'm an engineering student who, for some reason, wants to be a physician.

What I'm about

I'm a 20-year old student from Kentucky, and spend my time learning, researching potential cancer treatments, helping fundraise for the American Cancer Society, playing tennis, working on small web projects, and amateur photography.

Combined Passions

As a biomedical engineer, computer science major, and pre-medical student, I have a range of experiences to draw upon, and want to use the skills I have developed in order to help people in a meaningful way.

Easy to work with

I'm capable of working with, and leading, groups of all sizes. I make sure that my documentation is detailed and specific enough to help anyone get started. I'm open to feedback and criticism

I love innovation and admire creativity. As a future physician, I want to take my background and apply it to a field that is in constant need of innovation, with a little bit of creativity. My passion for problem-solving drives me to finding lasting solutions to current problems.
